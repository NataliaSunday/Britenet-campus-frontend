About us

We are family company made by our great grandfather Jhonny Bean. He was a bean lover and teach us how to take care about our little bean'ies. He used to talking with his beans and let them grow and grow until they become like sweet candies. He was our mentor - for me and Tonny, my little brother.

Our granfather died but he left us his small bean paradise.

Today we sell 1 000 beanies every year. Our company is the leader in bean market. In this place young an dynamic employees collect beans every day! In this year we started examine beans more and more to make them special...and we did! Today you can buy beans that can change your life!
